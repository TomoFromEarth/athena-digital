## Overview

Tomo splits his time between writing, recording, touring, and the dozens of small moments fans now expect to see online. The goal was not to chase every trend—it was to show up with consistency, authenticity, and a voice that matched how he already connects from stage.

Athena Digital mapped channels, themes, and practical posting windows around tour and studio life. Together we clarified what to say, when to post, and how community replies should feel so engagement stayed personal rather than generic.
